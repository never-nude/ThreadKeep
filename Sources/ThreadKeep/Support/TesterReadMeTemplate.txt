ThreadKeep __VERSION__

ThreadKeep is a private local archive for messages on this Mac.

How to open it:

1. Open this disk image.
2. Drag ThreadKeep into Applications.
3. Open ThreadKeep from Applications.

If macOS blocks it the first time:

- Control-click ThreadKeep in Applications, then choose Open.
- If macOS still blocks it, open System Settings > Privacy & Security and choose Open Anyway for ThreadKeep.

What to expect on first run:

- ThreadKeep opens to a welcome screen.
- You can choose whether conversation labels use contact names or stay as phone numbers.
- If you choose contact names, ThreadKeep will ask for Contacts access only when needed.
- ThreadKeep reads messages already available on this Mac. If your Messages history is not ready yet, open Messages once and let it finish loading before trying again.

What to try:

- Import all conversations or select just a few to start.
- Search inside a conversation for a name, place, or phrase.
- Export a conversation as a searchable PDF.

ThreadKeep keeps your archive local on this Mac unless you choose to export a file.
